# 📚 Fatelands

## Overview

**Fatelands** is a mobile idle text-adventure game with light pixel art visuals, where player choice shapes both the story and the fate of a fractured world. Designed as both a narrative experience and a data-driven system, the game blends branching storytelling, progression mechanics, and player-driven outcomes.

---

## 🧩 Story Premise

You awaken with no memory of who you are.

A lone postal worker discovers you unconscious and brings you back to their home. As you recover, you learn about the state of the world:

* A great conflict once raged between the forces of good and evil
* The Hero who stood against the darkness has mysteriously vanished
* In their absence, evil has spread and divided the land into isolated regions
* Entire sections of the world are now cut off—no travel, no communication… not even mail delivery

With no clear past and an uncertain future, your story begins with a choice:

👉 **Will you help restore the world… or walk away?**

---

## ⚔️ Core Gameplay

### 🧍 Character Creation

* Choose your avatar
* Name your character
* Define the beginning of your identity in a world that doesn’t remember you

---

### ❗ First Major Choice

* **Refuse to help** → Game ends early (with option to restart and choose differently)
* **Accept the call** → Begin your journey

This establishes the core theme: **your choices matter immediately and permanently.**

---

### 🗺️ World Progression

* The world is divided into **locked regions**
* Each region is controlled by hostile forces
* Players must:

  * Battle enemies
  * Grow stronger
  * Unlock new areas

As regions are restored:

* Postal routes reopen 📬
* NPCs regain connection
* The world begins to heal—or change

---

### 🧝 Combat & Progression

* Choose from different **weapons** early on
* Unlock:

  * Stronger enemies
  * New armor and gear
  * Unique abilities and powers

The game features **idle mechanics**, allowing progression over time even when the player is not actively playing.

---

### 📬 Reward System

Each region has its own postal worker.

When a region is restored:

* You receive rewards from the postal worker
* Rewards may include:

  * Equipment
  * Upgrades
  * Story insights

---

## 🌗 Choice & Alignment System

Throughout the game, players make decisions that influence their path:

* Act selflessly and restore balance → **Hero Path**
* Make darker, self-serving choices → **Villain Path**

By the end of the game, the player may become:

* The new Hero who reunites the world
* Or the Villain who takes control of it

---

## 🎮 Key Features

* Idle progression system
* Branching narrative with meaningful choices
* Region-based world unlocking
* Equipment and power progression
* Multiple endings (Hero vs Villain)
* Lightweight pixel-style visuals

## 🧠 Data Engineering Component (Portfolio Focus)

Fatelands is not just a game—it is also designed as a **data engineering project**.

The system tracks and processes player interactions to simulate real-world data pipelines.

### 📊 Data Collected

* Player choices and branching paths
* Progression metrics (levels, regions unlocked)
* Session duration and idle behavior
* Combat outcomes

---

### ⚙️ Data Pipeline

* Event data captured from gameplay
* Stored as raw structured data (JSON)
* Processed into analytical datasets
* Loaded into a database for querying

---

### 📈 Analytics Goals

* Identify common player decision paths
* Analyze where players quit or restart
* Balance difficulty based on progression data
* Compare Hero vs Villain outcomes
